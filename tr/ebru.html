Kaydır

"A dance of pigments upon the water, captured in a single breath of time."

Ebru (Marbling Art) is the delicate dance of color upon water, a profound classical art form where the fluidity of nature meets the intention of the artisan. Every pattern is entirely unique and impossible to replicate.

Natural earth pigments, crushed and mixed with ox gall, are rhythmically sprinkled over a basin of thickened water. The colors expand and embrace, orchestrated softly by the artist’s tools, before being immortalized forever on a single sheet of paper.
