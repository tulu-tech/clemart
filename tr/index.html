The Atelier

Not Made.
Authored.

ClemArt was born from a conviction — art deserves more than a frame. It deserves a dialogue. Our atelier draws from the most refined classical traditions of Ottoman illumination, reimagining them through a contemporary luxury lens.

Each piece passes through the hands of a master artisan. From the first cut of seasoned hardwood to the final burnish of 23.75 karat gold leaf, over forty steps shape each frame. No automation. No compromise.

Meet the Founder

Ayşe
Master Artisan

With decades of devotion to the classical arts, Ayşe founded ClemArt to preserve and reimagine centuries of Ottoman craftsmanship. Every ClemArt piece begins in her atelier, where gold leaf meets seasoned hardwood, and six centuries of tradition are distilled into a single, extraordinary frame.
